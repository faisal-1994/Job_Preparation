Finite Verb

Finite Verb দ্বারা বাক্যের Tense, Person, এবং Number নির্ণয় করা যায়।

Main Verb

Transitive Verb

Definition: যে Verb এর পরে Object থাকে।
Example: He reads a book.

Intransitive Verb

Definition: যে Verb এর পরে Object থাকে না।
Example: Birds fly.

Linking Verb

Definition: Subject কে Complement এর সাথে যুক্ত করে।
Example: She is happy.

Causative Verb

Definition: অন্য কাউকে দিয়ে কাজ করানো বোঝায়।
Example: He made me cry.

Auxiliary Verb

To Be Verb

is, am, are, was, were, be, being, been
Example: He is a student.

To Do Verb

do, does, did
Example: I do my homework.

To Have Verb

have, has, had
Example: She has a car.

Modal Verb

can, could, may, might, must, should, will, would
Example: You should study.
