Non Finite Verb

Non Finite Verb দ্বারা বাক্যের Tense নির্ণয় করা যায় না।

Gerund

v+ing = As Noun

যে সকল v+ing যুক্ত শব্দ বাক্যে noun এর কাজ করে সেগুলোই হল Gerund. একে Verbal Noun ও বলা হয়।

Example: Ringing is annoying

Infinitive

to + Verb = noun/adjective/adverb

যখন Verb এর পূর্বে to বসে এবং বাক্যে noun/adjective/adverb এর কাজ করে, তাকে Infinitive বলে।
যেমন: to eat, to play, to read

Example: I want to play football.

Participle

verb + ing , past form of verb (v2), having + past participle of verb(v3) = Adjective

যখন verb + ing বাক্যে adjective এর কাজ করে, তাকে Participle বলে।
তিন ধরনের:
(i) Present Participle (v+ing), (ii) Past Participle (v3) এবং (iii) perfect participle(having + v3)

Present Participle
v + ing = adjective

Example:
Ringing is annoying
Running water is useful.

Past Participle
past participle form of verb = adjective

Example:
A broken glass was on the table.
The fallen leaves covered the ground.

Perfect Participle
having + past participle form of verb(v3) = adjective

এটা দ্বারা সম্পন্ন হওয়া কোনো কাজকে বুঝায়।
such as: Having completed , Having eaten

Example:
Having completed the task, she feels relaxed.
Having slept well, she felt fresh.
Having finished his homework, he went to play.
Having eaten dinner, they will start the meeting.
